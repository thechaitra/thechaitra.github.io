Story Driven by Bernadette Jiwa

From the minute we are born, we participate in the rat race called life. As we grow older, we became more aware of this race and increasingly come under pressure to surge ahead of others. The lives we lead thus are all based on competitiveness, standing apart from the crowd, and emerging as the winner. But how effective is it truly? 

Story Driven, by Bernadette Jiwa, addresses the problem of conveying a message and living a life that is centered around oneself, instead of others. The book provides a framework to build a life doing the things one loves and crafting one's desired future by being authentic and real to oneself, as many successful people have themselves. This authenticity, according to the author, is the key to achieving greatness in all that one does.

"Our collective prosperity depends on each of us taking that first small step toward owning our story and the unique contribution we can make. " @bernadettejiwa

Click to Tweet

By using ourselves and our stories as our compass and North Star, we will be able to break ourselves away from the "survival mode" that all of us are in, by default. With well-written anecdotes and a riveting social commentary,  Story Driven is a compelling read that is guaranteed to turn around your business and your life.
